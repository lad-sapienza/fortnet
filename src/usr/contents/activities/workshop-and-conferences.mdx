## Workshop and conferences organised by the FortNet Project

<ActivityItem

Seminar by Richard Hodges at Sapienza University of Rome discussing the Mediterranean crossroads of Butrint, featuring presentations of two recent volumes on the late Roman and Middle Byzantine archaeology of the site.

Round table on innovation and potential of the Laser Aided Profiler, bringing together researchers from multiple Italian universities to share experiences and discuss best practices, standardization, and future perspectives of this archaeological tool.

Seminar by François Quantin at Sapienza University of Rome presenting the Albanian-French archaeological mission's research at Apollonia of Illyria, organized within the Classical Archaeology Seminar series of the Sapienza PhD School in Archaeology.
